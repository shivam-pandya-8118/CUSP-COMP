C. U. Shah Govt. Polytechnic

Introduction

       Established in June 1984, C. U. Shah Government Polytechnic, Wadhwan City, is a premier institute offering quality education, research, and training in engineering and technology. Named after Sheth Shri C. U. Shah, it is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU), Ahmedabad, and governed by the Department of Technical Education, Gujarat State, Gandhinagar. The well-planned campus is located near GIDC and Boda Talav, providing state-of-the-art facilities such as departmental buildings, administrative offices, hostels, and residential quarters for staff. The institute focuses on developing skilled engineers through strong academics and practical exposure to meet the evolving demands of the industry.

Produce competent diploma engineers for the society.

  • Impart technical knowledge and skills.
  • Inculcate professional values and make them socially responsible.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and employability skills.

Department of Computer Engineering

Introduction

       The Computer Engineering Department is Established in the year of 2001. In order to achieve task as stated in mission statement, we use learning tools like lecture-cum- discussion, laboratory work, tutorials, home assignments, seminars, field visits and project work. The department has well qualified faculty to cater the academic and other kinds of students' needs to enforce mission and vision of the department. Department possesses classrooms and prime laboratories as per AICTE norms and standards. We offer Interactive and effective learning environment to the students.

Produce competent diploma computer engineers for the society.

  • Provide outcome based quality education.
  • Inculcate soft skill and ethical values.
  • Motivate entrepreneurship and employability skills.

  1. Students will be able to apply knowledge of basic science, mathematics and computer science in their professional field.
  2. Students will be able to fulfil meets of software industry, understand technical specification, design and provide optimal solutions which are beneficial for society.
  3. Students will practice the profession with ethics, morals and leadership to relate engineering to welfare.
  4. Students will be able to adapt to the evolving technical challenges and changing career opportunities.
